Research Study: Fitbit Data Authorization

Welcome! Thank you for participating in our research study.

To share your Fitbit data with our research team, please click the button below. You will be redirected to Fitbit's website where you can authorize data sharing.

What data will be shared:
  • Profile information (name, age, gender, etc.)
  • Activity data (steps, distance, calories, active minutes)
  • Heart rate data (resting heart rate, heart rate zones)
  • Sleep data (sleep stages, sleep duration, sleep quality)
  • Breathing rate / Respiratory rate
  • Heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Body composition (weight, BMI, body fat percentage)
  • Oxygen saturation (SpO2)
  • Temperature and skin temperature
  • Nutrition data (food logs, water intake)
  • Location data (if applicable)
  • Social data (friends, leaderboards)
  • Settings and device information
Your data will be kept confidential and used only for research purposes.
